Billing Dispute Playbook

All invoice disputes must be acknowledged within 1 business day. Standard-priority disputes have a target resolution SLA of 3 business days after acknowledgement. Enterprise-priority disputes must be resolved within 24 hours. Required evidence for triage includes invoice ID, transaction reference, and the disputed contract line item. If a dispute remains unresolved after the SLA window, it is escalated to finance-oncall and customer success leadership in the same day.
